## 7. Review Disposition Model

The human independent reviewer must select exactly one final disposition from
the following four categories:

| Final disposition | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Approved for validation-execution reauthorization planning | All review criteria are Pass, no material contradiction remains, and the exact execution method may proceed only to a separate future validation-execution reauthorization checkpoint |
| Changes required | One or more review criteria require bounded execution-method revision; validation execution, policy adoption, and release decision remain unauthorized |
| Rejected | The execution method contains a material governance conflict, non-evidence violation, unresolvable contradiction, or unsafe evidence boundary; it must not proceed toward reauthorization planning |
| Unresolved | The reviewer cannot safely assess one or more material criteria without prohibited material or unsupported inference; it must not proceed toward reauthorization planning |
